ABOUT:
THE SPECS:

Trippy Lions Club is a collection of 5,555 Trippy NFTs—unique digital collectibles living on the Ethereum blockchain. Your Trippy Lion value increases as your Trippy Club membership card grants access to members only activities and perks that can be unlocked by the community through roadmap activation.

Each Trippy Lion is unique and programmatically generated from over 40 possible traits, including expressions, headwear, clothing, and much more. All lions are fire, but some are rarer than others.
The lions are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Ethereum blockchain and hosted on IPFS. Purchasing a Trippy Lion costs 0.1 ETH.

WELCOME TO THE JUNGLE!

When you buy a Trippy Lion, you’re not simply buying an NFT or a rare piece of art. You are also gaining membership access to a club whose benefits and offerings will increase over time. Your Trippy Lion can serve as your digital identity, and open digital doors for you.
