What is City Outlaw?

City Outlaws is an NFT based play to earn game built around the concept of street racing within major cities across the globe. The game takes advantage of the outlawed nature of street racing to create exciting racing events and a ton of rewards with the gamification economics built into it. The game is MMORPG in nature but also has single player online and offline modes that all feed into the game economics.

The Game and Gameplay

Gameplay

The game is set out in the metaverse with racing veneues as virtual represenataion of actual cities across the world like Paris, Lagos, Miami, Karacas, Doha, Munich, Rio, Nairobi, Tokyo etc. Each race is held in a specific city and your race card if tied to that city gives you added advantage, like a home advantage (more on this under gamification). The gameplay is broken down into racing events, time trails, stunts, drag racing and racing missions.

Racing Events.

There are racing events setup by the City Outlaws team every 24 hours with different game rewards. Some the racing events are for drivers to win prizes and others allow drivers win NFT game assets that can be sold on any marketplace. These events can involve from 50 drivers at a time and above and can either be free to enter or attract a small fee. However, no one can participate in a racing event without having a City Outlaws race card NFT.

Gamification

- Win races and earn in-game NFTs
- Win special missions and earn bluechip NFTs.
- Race and earn TOLLS as reward for winning/time spent.
- Rent out your Car.
- Partner with a Pro Driver.
- Create and sell your own in-game NFT assets.
